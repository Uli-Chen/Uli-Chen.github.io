Hi, my name is Chen, a third-year undergraduate at the College of Computer Science and Technology, Jilin University, where I conduct research in the [MIC Lab](https://micxyb.github.io/) supervised by **Professor Yuanbo Xu**. 
Previously, my research centered on Data Mining (DM) and Recommender Systems (RS). I am now focusing on Generative Modeling, with a particular emphasis on Flow Matching (FM) and Large Language Models (LLMs). Currently, I plan to explore the intersection of RS and LLMs.

If you are interested in collaborating or have any questions about my work, please feel free to reach out via [email](mailto:uicetomeetn@gmail.com).
